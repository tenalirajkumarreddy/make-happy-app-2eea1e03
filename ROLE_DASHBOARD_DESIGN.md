# Role-Based Dashboard Design

## Overview
Each role should see a dashboard tailored to their specific responsibilities and daily workflows.

---

## 1. SUPER_ADMIN Dashboard

### Primary Focus: Business Overview & Management
**What they do:** Oversee entire business, manage users, warehouses, settings

### Key Metrics:
- Global business metrics (all warehouses)
- Staff activity monitoring
- System alerts
- Management functions

### Dashboard Components:
1. **Stats Row 1:** Today's Sales, Cash in Hand, Active Staff
2. **Stats Row 2:** Total Customers, Total Stores, Pending Handovers
3. **Quick Actions:** Manage Users, Staff Directory, Warehouse, Settings, Reports
4. **Charts:** Sales Trend (7 days), Staff Activity List
5. **Alerts Section:** Low stock, overdue outstanding, missing handovers

---

## 2. MANAGER Dashboard

### Primary Focus: Operations & Team Management
**What they do:** Manage warehouse operations, staff, inventory, customer relations

### Key Metrics:
- Warehouse-specific sales
- Cash held (my account)
- Staff holdings (pending handovers)
- Pending orders
- Low stock items

### Dashboard Components:
1. **Stats Row 1:** Today's Sales, Cash Held, Staff Holdings
2. **Stats Row 2:** Pending Orders, Low Stock, Needs Attention
3. **Quick Actions:** Record Sale, Review Handovers, Manage Staff, View Reports
4. **Sales by Staff:** Bar chart showing today's sales per staff member
5. **Pending Handovers:** List of staff with cash to handover
6. **Inventory Alerts:** Low stock warnings with reorder buttons

---

## 3. AGENT Dashboard

### Primary Focus: Daily Sales & Route Management
**What they do:** Visit stores on routes, record sales, collect payments

### Key Metrics:
- My sales today
- Cash on hand (handover reminder)
- Stores visited vs remaining
- Next store with distance

### Dashboard Components:
1. **Stats Row:** My Sales Today, Cash on Hand
2. **Route Progress:** Stores visited / remaining, Next store
3. **Quick Actions (BIG buttons):** Record Sale, My Route, Stores
4. **Route Progress List:** Stores with visit status and sale amounts
5. **Weekly Performance:** Sales by day vs target
6. **Offline Queue:** Sync status for queued sales

---

## 4. MARKETER Dashboard

### Primary Focus: Store Acquisition & Marketing
**What they do:** Find new stores, onboard customers, marketing campaigns

### Key Metrics:
- New stores today
- Leads visited
- Conversion rate
- Sales generated by my referrals
- Pending KYC

### Dashboard Components:
1. **Stats Row 1:** New Stores Today, Leads Today, Conversion Rate
2. **Stats Row 2:** Sales Generated, Pending KYC
3. **Quick Actions:** Add New Store, View My Stores, Scan QR Code
4. **My Stores List:** Top performers with sales and status
5. **Leads Pipeline:** Prospects by stage
6. **KYC Status:** Stores needing document upload

---

## 5. POS Dashboard

### Primary Focus: Counter Sales & Walk-in Customers
**What they do:** Handle walk-in sales, manage inventory at counter

### Key Metrics:
- Today's sales
- Items sold
- Popular products
- Current stock levels

### Dashboard Components:
1. **Stats:** Today's Sales, Items Sold, Walk-in Customers
2. **Quick Actions:** New Sale, View Inventory, Reports
3. **Popular Products:** Top selling items today
4. **Low Stock Alert:** Items needing restock
5. **Recent Transactions:** Last 10 sales with details

---

## 6. OPERATOR Dashboard

### Primary Focus: Production & Manufacturing
**What they do:** Manage production, raw materials, manufacturing costs

### Key Metrics:
- Today's production
- Raw material usage
- Batch completion rate
- Quality metrics

### Dashboard Components:
1. **Stats:** Today's Production, Raw Materials Used, Active Batches
2. **Quick Actions:** New Production Batch, View Inventory, Quality Check
3. **Production Schedule:** Upcoming batches
4. **Material Alerts:** Low raw materials
5. **Quality Metrics:** Pass/fail rates

---

## 7. CUSTOMER Dashboard

### Primary Focus: Order History & Account Management
**What they do:** Place orders, view history, track deliveries

### Key Metrics:
- Outstanding balance
- Pending orders
- Order history

### Dashboard Components:
1. **Stats:** Outstanding Balance, Pending Orders, Total Orders
2. **Quick Actions:** Place Order, View History, Make Payment
3. **Recent Orders:** Last 5 orders with status
4. **Payment History:** Recent payments
5. **Available Products:** Catalog with pricing

---

## Implementation Strategy

### Phase 1: Current Dashboard (ALL roles see same view)
- Shows warehouse stats
- Action center buttons
- Charts
- Pending orders

### Phase 2: Role-Based Split
- Create separate dashboard components for each role
- Route to correct dashboard based on role
- Share common components (stats cards, charts)

### Phase 3: Role-Specific Enhancements
- Add role-specific data queries
- Customize quick actions per role
- Add role-specific alerts and notifications
